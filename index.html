Why will you enjoy your Cuba vacation?

Cuba has always been attractive. I love her for her uniqueness, creativity and spirit of survival. I love Cuba because despite 60 years of failure, it remains an optimistic and open place. Walk down the street with a Cuban friend and in one block you'll receive five handshakes, four kisses, three "Dime Hermano!" And at least two invitations to someone's home.
